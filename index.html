Artifact 3.4 — LLM Training Process & Costs

This artifact visualizes the end-to-end LLM training pipeline (data sourcing → engineering → pretraining → alignment → evaluation → deployment) and maps each stage to the major resource categories—data, compute, energy, time, and people/tooling. It highlights cost drivers such as token scale, GPU class/cluster topology, and labeling/alignment cycles, and includes representative model families to situate the discussion. A Sankey view summarizes typical cost distribution ranges (illustrative), while a final panel lists pragmatic optimization levers (efficient attention, mixed precision, curriculum, eval gates, quantization). The goal is to help stakeholders understand where cost and time accrue and which levers most effectively trade expense for capability, quality, and safety.
